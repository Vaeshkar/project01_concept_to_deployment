About Us

We are the first animal salon in Germany, founded in 1960. At Fluffy Glow, we believe our fluffy friends deserve the same luxurious care as their humans. Our co-founders, Dennis and Phoebe, are passionate animal lovers. Dennis shares his life with a golden retriever, a pony, and two ducks, while Phoebe dreams of owning her own animal farm when she retires!
